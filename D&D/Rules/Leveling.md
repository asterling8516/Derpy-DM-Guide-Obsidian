### 💡 The Big Picture

- **The Level 10 Milestone:** To reach Level 10, your party must have earned enough XP to kill **14.2 Adult Red Dragons**.
    
- **The Final Push:** To get from Level 19 to Level 20, you need **50,000 XP**, which is **11.1 dragons** just for that one single level!
    
- **The Grind:** If you fought one Adult Red Dragon every single session, it would take you **nearly 80 sessions** to reach Level 20. Milestone leveling usually gets you there much faster by focusing on your epic deeds rather than the body count.
