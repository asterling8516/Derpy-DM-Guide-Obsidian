# Story Version

The ruins did not just challenge your bodies; they attacked your minds.

As you pushed north, the present faded, replaced by fragments of the past. And in the shadows of your history... there was Zalost.

Jersey, you watched your father strike a bargain with them, a deal where Zalost seemed to gain nothing... yet.

Fynn, you drifted in a void, listening to a dispute over your very soul—a war between your creator and Asmodeus.

Garp, you were back in Shadows Haven, staggering through the streets guided to Coralie, the catalyst for your redemption.

Kookush, the peace of Frostlight shattered. Rumors of the Iron Law you broke you, forcing you to flee or face judgment.

And Neighya... you felt the magic surge. You heard the voice offering control. You felt the golden shackles snap shut as you were pulled into the lamp, a prisoner of your own power.

The weight of these memories crushed you, forcing you to rest. But the dungeon offered no peace.

Crystal spiders descended. In the chaos, Neighya managed to befriend one, dubbing the creature "Princess." But exhaustion haunted the party, especially Fynn.

Your sleep was shattered by a massive explosion. Investigating the debris, Jersey attempted to open a crate... by punching it. The resulting blast sent shrapnel flying, injuring poor Princess.

Battered but moving, you navigated a gauntlet of elemental pressure plates. You freed chests encased in crystal—Garp with a well-placed shot, and Kookush finally teaching Jersey how a crowbar actually works.

Even when the crystals tried to seize you, trapping you in the stone, your reflexes saved you.

Now, you stand at a crossroads. The way forward is unclear... but the clock continues to tick.
