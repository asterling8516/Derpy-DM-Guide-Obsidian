Last session, the frozen tundra demanded a heavy toll.

Amidst the brutal clash with the Frost Giants and their mammoths, Adamson began to hear the forest whispering. At first, the voices were lost in the roar of battle, but soon they cut through the chaos, clear and urgent, urging you all to flee into the trees. They offered sanctuary. They offered an escape.

The retreat began. Adamson reached out with magic, calming one of the raging mammoths, while Frezer warped through space to pull allies from the giants' warpath. Safety was just steps away.

But as the giants prepared to give chase, Cinderella made a fatal choice. Standing her ground, the bold Dragonborn taunted the massive brutes, drawing their fury. The giants stopped in their tracks and turned their massive axes upon her.

It was a merciless barrage. Knocked unconscious in the snow, Cinderella suffered two brutal, consecutive strikes. As the chaos paused, fate demanded its toll. She failed her final death save. Drawing her last breath in the biting cold, Cinderella died.

In a desperate, clutch moment, Adamson commanded his charmed mammoth to scoop up her lifeless body, carrying her into the safety of the trees. The forest immediately reacted to your arrival. Vines and massive thorns erupted from the earth, acting as a living wall that sliced the raging giants to ribbons until they finally fell dead.

Following Adamson’s lead, you navigated the treacherous woods, arriving at a clearing. There stood a small, two-story cottage ringed by strange dirt mounds. Adamson knocked, and the door was answered by "Mother."

But she was not as she once was. She was frail, greatly aged. She revealed that since Adamson had left, she had abandoned the dark rituals that prolonged her youth. She was fading, allowing nature to finally take its course.

You delivered your message on behalf of _Happily Ever After_, seeking a way to contact Ursula regarding the attacks on the capitals. Mother’s response brought a chilling realization: you were not the first. Another group had already come asking the exact same questions just days prior.

Desperate, you asked if her magic could save Cinderella. Mother admitted she could... but the price was a century of lifespan. It was a cost neither she nor the party could afford to pay. Heavy-hearted, you gathered Cinderella's belongings, leaving her body in Mother's care to finally rest.

Confused by the existence of a rival party and mourning your fallen friend, you set your sights on a small village two days away. From there, the path leads back to Frostlight. Back to Piper. Back for answers.

And that... is where we find ourselves today.
