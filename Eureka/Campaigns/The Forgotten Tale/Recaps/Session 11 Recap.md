You watched as Stove died hearing some strange conversation between two people Zalost and someone named Jersey. Zalost warned that if he was killed others would also die. After sleepy Sleepy began spiraling because of the loss of his friend. Yelling at children crying looking for their parents. After a short while you decided to head to Infernium. Along the way ran into a mass of statues whom you were able to figure out were alive, but turned into statues. A woman yelled from a nearby worn down cottage to get inside away from the strange creatures that were hiding in the low hanging mist. After some debate on what to do she closed the door and said good luck surviving. Running to the cottage you were attacked by strange chickens that were turning you to stone. Sleepy was the first to arrive at the cottage and demanded to be let in, but the woman's fear was to much. You were able to defeat the creatures and sleepy made threats to the woman which caused her to take off towards Mistveil.
