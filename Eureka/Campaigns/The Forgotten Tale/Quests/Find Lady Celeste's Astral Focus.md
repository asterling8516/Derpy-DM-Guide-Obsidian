# Who
a Archfey named "Lady Celeste"

# What
Find [[Lady Celeste]]'s [[Astral Focus]] that was stolen from her. She believes it was taken to [[The Kingdom of Spades]]. When you asked how to contact her once you've found it she told you that you "merely need let it see the sky and I will find you".

# Why
She offered you transportation for your troubles. Gifting you a carriage and 4 Tumblesteeds for your agreeing to help her. A fae pact was formed.
